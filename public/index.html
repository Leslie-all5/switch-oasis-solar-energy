About Switch Oasis

Established in 2020, Switch Oasis is a premier solar energy solutions provider headquartered in Ghana. With a firm commitment to quality, reliability, and innovation, we specialize in customized solar installations, hybrid energy systems, electrical integration, and long-term system support.

We've built a solid reputation as a trusted energy partner, delivering clean, scalable solutions across Ghana, with growing international clients in the US, UK, Germany, and beyond. Our growth is fueled by referrals, repeat clients, and premium components sourced from Ghana and China.

Our Vision

To become Africa's benchmark in renewable energy solutions, making clean power accessible, efficient, and dependable for all.

Our Mission

  • Deliver world-class solar solutions tailored to client needs
  • Use only premium, certified, performance-guaranteed components
  • Maintain a client-focused approach from consultation to aftercare
  • Promote sustainable living and Africa's shift to renewable energy
